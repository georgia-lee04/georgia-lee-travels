About me

About me

I'm Georgia, a 24-year old living just outside of London - like many others I have a huge love for travel which was especially sparked by first solo trip last summer to Jordan as part of a group tour.

I studied at the University of Nottingham obtaining a BA (Hons) degree in History, followed by an MSc Psychology degree. Whilst still finishing off my final dissertation I began working part time with the Alternative Hair Show, which then soon turned into a full time position and expanding to involve a position at Sanrizz simulateously. Both organisations are linked under the same ownership with the Alternative Hair Show being an annual charity event in aid of fighting leukaemia and Sanrizz, a hair company with a legacy of over 40 years at the forefront of British Hairdressing and Education. I really enjoyed my work in both roles with highlights including being part of the small team to organise and coordinate the Alternative Hair Show at the ExCel London (2023) and the Roundhouse in London (2024), writing press releases to share with industry magazines and getting to manage social media content and work on marketing campaigns.

From this I've taken some time to figure out my next direction and have upskilled myself through completing the SheCodes Bootcamp course over 5 months, through which I have learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and how to use frameworks such as Bootstrap. I also got the chance to work with API integration, AI technology as well as learning responsive design.

This website/blog is a way to share my new love for coding as well as my love for travel and writing which I hope to keep adding to as I embark on more exciting new trips and adventures!
